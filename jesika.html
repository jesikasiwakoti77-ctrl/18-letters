Happy 18th birthday sisy 🤍

It feels like yesterday you were that 12-year-old girl I first met ,
a little stubborn, a little shy, and full of dreams.
And now look at you… 18, stronger, wiser,
about to conquer your boards and step into a whole new chapter of life .

We’ve had our share of fights and silent treatment moments.
But no matter how loud the arguments were,
our bond has always been louder.
You matter to me more than you’ll ever truly know.

I know I’m always worrying too much, always caring a little extra.
You get angry quickly, you act tough, you stay quiet instead of explaining your feelings.
But that’s just you. And I’ve learned to understand that.

Watching you grow has been one of my favorite things in life.
I’m so proud of your strength, your heart, and the way you’re becoming your own person.

No matter where life takes you, remember I’ll always be right here.
Supporting you, protecting you, and loving you in my own imperfect but real way.

Happy 18th, my forever little sister.
This world better be ready for you ✨
I LOVE YOU MORE THAN YOU COULD EVER THINK
